SINGAPORE AND MALAYSIA PACKAGE

Tour Overview

Day 01

Arrive Kula Lumpur capital of Malaysia @ 0801 hrs, upon arrival meet and greet by our local representative and en route to Genting High Land Battu Cave visit with Lunch at Indian Restaurant then proceed to Genting High land by two way cable car and check in the Hotel ,leisure at the hotel , Vist Casino at your Own , Dinner at Genting in Indian Restaurarnt. Over night stay at Hotel.(B,L,D)

Day 02

After Breakfast , Check out Hotel Keep the luggage's’ in the lobby and proceed to enjoy outdoor theme park up to 12.00 noon, then proceed to Kulalambur city, Lunch at Indian Restaurant ,visit Battu Caves ,the famous Murugan Temple, Chokalate sales outlet , check in hotel and leisure activities at your own. Dinner at Indian Restaurant. (B,L,D)

Day 03

After breakfast city Tour starts ,sight seeing of KL covering major tourist attraction like Twin tower and tallest communication tower of South Asia ,National Museum,The King’s Palace , National Mosque,Merdeka Square, during city Tour Lunch at Indian Restaurant and others. Dinner at Indian Restaurant and Overnight stay at KL Hotel (B,L,D)

Day 04

Check out the Hotel and assemble in the lobby by 06.00.a.m, for breakfast ,get ready to proceed to Singapore by a/c coach. Arrive Singapore by 01.30.p.m., after lunch check in the hotel and evening proceed to Night safari of Singapore, Dinner at Indian restaurant , overnight stay at Hotel.( B,L,D)

Day 05

After breakfast proceed for local sight seeing of Singapore covering official and tourist attractions of Singapore,( Small Cruise Travel upto Merlion at your own ) then proceed for Lunch at the Hotel and Proceed to Sentosa Sunset (Admission + 1 way cable car ride + Under water World + Dolphin Lagoon + Songs of the Sea). Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel. (B, L, D)

Day 06

After Breakfast Check out the Hotel with luggages at 08.00.a.m, Free for shopping and temple visits at your own, Lunch at Indian Restaurant nearest to the Mustafa Shopping Mall. After shopping assemble at 11.30.a.m, at the specified landmark area to proceed to Airport and check in at Airport by 01.30.p.m., to catch the Srilankan airways and flight departs by 04.30p.m., and reaches Chennai by 07.55.p.m. Flight(B/L),Dinner in the flight.Reached Chennai
